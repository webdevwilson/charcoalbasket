Struggling to keep the temperature up in your charcoal smoker? Running out of fuel too quickly?

Look no further than our charcoal basket. No charcoal smoker is complete without one. Our baskets are the best way to maintain long and consistent burns with your smoker. Using the Minion Method you will be able to easily maintain 6-8 hour burns without refilling. This allows for you to maintain a constant temperature over long periods of time within your cooking chamber for optimal smoking conditions.
