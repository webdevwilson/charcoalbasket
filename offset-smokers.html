Tips for measuring your firebox for a charcoal basket:

  • Allow around 4 inches or more around the basket. It shouldn't be too snug, there should be some air circulation.
  • Remember you have to be able to insert the basket through your door or other opening. Don't make it so big you can't get it in (yes, it's happened before).
  • Cut out a cardboard box or similar to test your measurements
